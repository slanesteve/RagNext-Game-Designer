# AI Co-Author & Generation Assistants

RagNext is equipped with a premium, state-of-the-art suite of built-in AI tools. These assistants integrate directly with leading AI models like **Google Gemini** and image synthesis engines like **Pollinations.ai** to accelerate your game development workflow.

---

## 🤖 Visual Graph AI Assistant

Tired of drawing nodes manually? The visual editor features a complete natural-language graph generator:

1. **Describe Your Scene**: Open the AI Assistant panel on the canvas and describe what you want in plain text (e.g. *"If the player has the rusty key, play a unlock sound, display 'You opened the door', and teleport to the Corridor. Otherwise play a locked sound and display 'It is locked'."*).
2. **Review Canvas Changes**: The assistant compiles your text into functional nodes in real-time, placing them on your graph canvas with temporary dashed borders.
3. **Accept or Revert**: Inspect the generated logic paths. Keep them with a single click, or revert if you want to rewrite your prompt.

---

## 📝 AI Narrative Co-Author

Create rich, immersive room and character descriptions instantly using the **Zen Compose Overlay**:

* **One-Click Refinement**: Select any text box in the editor details pane and click the `📝 Compose` button to launch a distraction-free fullscreen writing workspace.
* **Co-Author Pipeline**: Click the **AI Co-Author** button to expand your story outline, fix pacing, or add rich stylistic descriptions based on your writing style guidelines.
* **Direct Gemini Integration**: Connects straight to the Google Gemini API (recommending `gemini-1.5-pro` for deep narrative logic) using your private Google AI Studio API key.

---

## 🖼️ Portrait Image Generation

Generate custom room layouts, character portraits, and item artwork on the fly:

* **Seamless Sidebar Assignment**: Generate premium graphical assets directly within your media catalog sidebar.
* **Pollinations.ai Integration**: Tap into free, high-speed image generators. Configure custom style rules (e.g., *"Oil painting style"*, *"Cyberpunk neon vector art"*) inside workspace preferences.
* **Drag-and-Drop Porting**: Drag generated artwork straight from the asset library and drop it onto rooms, characters, or items to instantly skin your game world.
